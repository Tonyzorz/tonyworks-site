Milestones reward normal play

Achievements turn repeated runs into account-wide progress. Enemy defeats, boss victories, regional exploration, and other milestones can grant permanent stat bonuses or unlock characters. They are not intended to replace the main run strategy; instead, they reward players for reaching meaningful goals while learning the game's systems.

The mode tabs distinguish Normal, Hard, and requirements that can be completed in either mode. Search by goal or reward when deciding which objective is close enough to pursue without abandoning a stronger route.

Prioritize reachable rewards

An achievement is most efficient when its requirement overlaps with what the current build already wants to do. A boss goal is easier to justify during a run prepared for that region, while a defeat-count milestone can progress naturally during equipment farming. Chasing an unrelated objective can consume Adventure Points without improving the current boss plan.

Permanent rewards compound with equipment collection and owned-character bonuses. Small bonuses become meaningful across many systems, but the exact reward should remain visible so players can judge its value without guesswork.
