FAQ

Common questions about Infinite Loot-Loop.

What is Infinite Loot-Loop?

A mobile roguelike RPG. You loop through zones fighting monsters, collecting loot, and beating bosses to extend your run ??chasing rarer gear and higher levels each time.

How do Battle Points (BP) work?

You start with 30 BP. A win costs 1, dying costs 4 (or 6 in Hard Mode), and each boss kill refunds 3. When BP hits 0 it's game over and your level/EXP/gold reset ??but a plain death does not reset those. See the Guide for the full loop.

Why won't a rare item drop for me?

Drop chances fall off sharply for items you already own copies of, and higher zone tiers reduce drop rates. Boost your odds with LUC, drop-rate gear/sets, and the drop-rate character. Boss uniques are ~1% (0.75% in Hard Mode) before bonuses.

How do I unlock characters?

Most characters are unlocked with gold or by completing specific achievements; two are premium (in-app purchase). Browse them on the Characters page.

What is Hard Mode?

A tougher variant with stronger bosses and higher death cost (6 BP), offering distinct Hard Mode drops. It's meant for well-geared runs.

Is my progress saved across devices?

Yes ??the game has cloud save, so your permanent progress follows your account.

Where does this wiki's data come from?

Every monster, boss, item, map and character page is generated directly from the game's own data, so it stays accurate to the current build.
