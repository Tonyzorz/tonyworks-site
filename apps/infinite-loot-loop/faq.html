What is Infinite Loot-Loop?

A mobile roguelike RPG. You loop through zones fighting monsters, collecting loot, and beating bosses to extend your run — chasing rarer gear and higher levels each time.

How do I move on a phone?

Touch and hold an empty part of the map to move toward your finger. Drag to change direction and release to stop. Touching buttons or an open menu will not move your character.

How do shop purchases work?

Gold purchases ask you to choose Purchase or Cancel before anything is charged. If you do not have enough gold, the purchase button is disabled.

How do Adventure Points (AP) work?

You start with 30 AP. A win costs 1, dying costs 4 (or 6 in Hard Mode), and each boss kill refunds 3. When AP hits 0 it's game over and your level/EXP/gold reset — but a plain death does not reset those. See the Guide for the full loop.

Why won't a rare item drop for me?

Drop chances fall off sharply for items you already own copies of. Boost your odds with LUC (a very small bonus, capped at +0.6%), drop-rate gear/sets, and the drop-rate character. Boss uniques are ~1% (0.75% in Hard Mode) before bonuses.

How do I unlock characters?

Most characters are unlocked with gold or by completing specific achievements; two are premium (in-app purchase). Browse them on the Characters page.

What is Hard Mode?

A tougher variant with stronger bosses and higher death cost (6 AP), offering distinct Hard Mode drops. It's meant for well-geared runs.

Is my progress saved across devices?

The game supports cloud save through its supported platform identity and device setup. Cross-device restore depends on using the same supported platform account; Android's current sign-in setup may remain device-local until platform authentication is configured.

Where does this wiki's data come from?

Every monster, boss, item, map and character page is generated directly from the game's own data, so it stays accurate to the current build.
