A run is a resource-management problem

Infinite Loot-Loop mixes map exploration with turn-based battles. Movement raises the encounter gauge, and an encounter consumes Adventure Points whether the battle is convenient or not. The challenge is therefore not simply to defeat everything. A strong run chooses fights that provide useful experience, gold, or equipment while preserving enough Adventure Points to reach the next boss.

Boss victories open routes and can restore momentum. Defeat ends the current run, but collected gear, unlocked equipment slots, achievements, and permanent collection bonuses make later attempts stronger. That combination of temporary growth and permanent preparation is the central loop.

How to use this wiki

Use the beginner guide for a complete explanation of controls and progression. Before entering a new region, review the world atlas and its boss connections. When a build feels weak, compare the item library, check complete equipment sets, and confirm which monsters can drop the pieces you need.

The catalog numbers are reference data, not a promise that every future version will remain unchanged. Balance updates can alter health, attack, prices, drops, or routes. The data methodology explains the export and review process, while patch notes record important player-facing changes.

Recommended first objective

Learn movement and the encounter gauge, equip every useful early drop, then reach the first boss with enough Adventure Points to complete the fight. Avoid grinding every enemy simply because it is nearby.
