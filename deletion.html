Data Deletion Request - Infinite Loot Loop

Last updated: 16 July 2026

This page explains how to delete the data associated with Infinite Loot Loop, operated by Tony Works (tonyzorz@naver.com). The Game does not require you to create a separate account; data is tied to your device, an anonymous player identifier, or your Apple Game Center ID.

Delete in the Game (fastest)

  1. Open Infinite Loot Loop.
  2. Go to Settings → Data.
  3. Tap Erase Data and confirm.

This permanently deletes your local gameplay save and your cloud gameplay save. If the device is offline, cloud deletion is queued and completes on the next online app launch. Store purchase and entitlement records are retained so eligible purchases can be restored.

To delete paid-entitlement records as well, use the email request below.

Delete local data only

Deleting or uninstalling the app removes all local save data from your device.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot open the Game, email tonyzorz@naver.com with:

  • Subject line: Data Deletion - Infinite Loot Loop.
  • The player ID shown in Settings → Data (if available).

We will delete your cloud save and entitlement records, typically within 30 days, and confirm by reply.

What an email deletion request removes

  • Cloud save data (level, items, achievements, map state, progression).
  • Paid-entitlement records (diamonds, Remove Ads, purchased characters, processed transaction IDs).

Advertising and diagnostic identifiers processed by Google (AdMob, Firebase) and Apple are governed by their own controls: reset your advertising ID in device settings and decline the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt. Purchase records held by the Apple App Store or Google Play are managed through those stores.
