What you will find here

Tony Works is the official home of Infinite Loot-Loop, a mobile roguelike RPG about planning efficient routes, defeating increasingly dangerous enemies, and turning collected equipment into permanent progress. This site is maintained alongside the game rather than assembled from third-party articles.

The companion wiki combines developer-written explanations with data exported from the Unity project. Players can learn how Adventure Points shape a run, compare character strengths, inspect item effects, trace connected maps, and prepare for bosses without relying on unexplained spreadsheets.

Tony Works also creates focused web experiences. Show Me the Saju provides a free, private Korean Four Pillars chart with no sign-up, available across 11 languages.

Where to begin

New players should start with the field guide. It explains combat, equipment, character growth, death, permanent collection bonuses, Hard Mode, and the first practical decisions in a run. Returning players can use the world atlas and item library as planning tools.

Every catalog page also includes a written strategy section explaining how to interpret its data. The game-data methodology describes what is exported automatically, what is reviewed manually, and where the numbers may differ from a future build.
