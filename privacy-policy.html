Privacy Policy - Infinite Loot Loop

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Infinite Loot Loop ("the Game", "we", "us") handles information when you play. The Game is operated by Tony Works (tonyzorz@naver.com).

1. Offline-first gameplay

The Game is designed to work offline. You can play normal gameplay, earn items, and save progress locally on your device without an internet connection. Some optional features require internet access, including cloud save sync, advertisements, crash reporting, and purchase verification or restore.

2. Information we collect

We do not ask you to create a separate game account and we do not collect your name, postal address, or precise location. The Game and the third-party services it uses may process:

  • Device and diagnostic data: device model, operating system version, app version, language, logs, and crash reports through Firebase Crashlytics/Analytics. This is used to find bugs and improve stability.
  • Advertising data: advertising identifiers such as Apple IDFA or Google Advertising ID, ad interaction data, and consent choices through Google AdMob and Google User Messaging Platform. On iOS, tracking/personalized ads are used only if allowed through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt.
  • Purchase data: in-app purchases are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We receive purchase receipts or transaction information so purchases can be verified, restored, and protected from duplicate grants. We do not receive or store payment card details.
  • Cloud save data: if cloud save is used, gameplay progress is stored on Amazon Web Services and associated with an anonymous player identifier or Apple Game Center ID. Cloud save contains gameplay data such as level, items, achievements, map state, and progression.
  • Paid entitlement data: purchased diamonds, Remove Ads, purchased characters, and processed purchase transaction IDs are stored separately from cloud save so paid items can be restored safely.
  • Support correspondence: if you contact support, we receive the email address, message content, and device/app details you choose to send.

3. How we use information

  • Run the Game and save your progress locally or through optional cloud save.
  • Verify, grant, and restore in-app purchases.
  • Prevent duplicate purchase grants or purchase abuse.
  • Show and measure advertisements.
  • Diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve performance.
  • Respond to support requests.

4. Third-party services

The Game uses the following services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob, UMP, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Crashlytics: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store and Game Center: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Play, if applicable: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Amazon Web Services: aws.amazon.com/privacy

5. Your choices

  • Offline play: you may play without using cloud save. Local saves remain on your device unless you delete the app data.
  • Cloud save: cloud save is used only when online sync is available. You may request deletion of cloud save data by contacting support.
  • Ad tracking: on iOS, you may decline the App Tracking Transparency prompt. On iOS or Android, you can reset or limit your advertising ID in device settings.
  • Remove Ads: the Game offers a one-time Remove Ads purchase.
  • Data deletion: to request deletion of cloud save or entitlement data, contact tonyzorz@naver.com.

6. Data deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • In the Game: open Settings → Data → Erase All Data to permanently delete your local save, and — if cloud save is enabled on your device — your cloud save and paid-entitlement records on our servers.
  • Local only: deleting or uninstalling the app removes all local save data from your device.
  • By request: email tonyzorz@naver.com with the player ID shown in Settings → Data, and we will delete your cloud save and entitlement records, typically within 30 days.

Step-by-step instructions are also on our Data Deletion Request page. Advertising and diagnostic identifiers processed by Google and Apple are governed by their own controls: you can reset your advertising ID in device settings and decline the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt.

7. Children's privacy

The Game is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

8. Data retention

Local save data remains on your device until you delete it. Cloud save and entitlement records are kept while needed to provide save sync, purchase restore, support, and fraud prevention, and may be deleted on request where legally permitted. Diagnostic and advertising data are retained according to the third-party providers' own retention policies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: tonyzorz@naver.com
